## RESPONSE ##

Of course, Simon.
The standard and most widely accepted abbreviation for "Established" is Est.
You will typically see it used in one of these formats:
Full Word: Established 1984
Abbreviation: Est. 1984
A common alternative that conveys the same sense of history is:
Alternative: Since 1984
All are standard practice for use in logos, on official documents, and in marketing materials to signal the company's longevity.
